Partnership Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical response time for inquiries? +

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. Technical inquiries receive detailed responses within 48 hours. Government partnership requests are prioritized and typically receive initial contact within 4-6 working hours.

Do you provide site visits and demonstrations? +

Yes, we offer multiple demonstration options:

  • Virtual online demos (available within 48 hours)
  • Site visits to our R&D center (scheduled weekly)
  • Visits to existing installations (subject to client approval)
  • On-site demonstrations at your location (for qualified projects)
What government certifications do you hold? +

We are certified and empaneled with:

  • MNRE (Ministry of New & Renewable Energy) Certified
  • UNIDO (United Nations Industrial Development Organization) Empaneled
  • UHS (Performance Testing) Validated
  • Various state government empanelments (GEDA, MEDA, etc.)
  • Defense establishment approved vendor
What is your project implementation timeline? +

Typical project timelines:

  • Small systems (up to 100 meals/day): 4-6 weeks
  • Medium systems (100-500 meals/day): 8-12 weeks
  • Large systems (500+ meals/day): 12-16 weeks
  • Custom/Industrial systems: 16-24 weeks
  • All timelines include survey, design, manufacturing, and installation
