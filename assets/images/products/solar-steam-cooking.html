How It Works

Four-step process for clean, solar-powered cooking

01

Solar Concentration

Precision reflectors concentrate sunlight onto a receiver, heating thermal fluid to 200-250°C.

02

Heat Transfer

Thermal fluid transfers heat to water in a heat exchanger, generating pressurized steam.

03

Steam Delivery

Pressurized steam is delivered to cooking vessels through insulated pipelines.

04

Cooking & Storage

Steam cooks food in specially designed vessels, with thermal storage for cloudy periods.
