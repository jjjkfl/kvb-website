Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum sunlight requirement? +
The system requires minimum 4-5 hours of direct sunlight per day. During monsoon or cloudy days, the hybrid backup system automatically takes over.
What maintenance is required? +
Annual inspection and cleaning of reflectors. The system is designed for minimal maintenance with automated operation.
What is the installation timeline? +
Typically 4-6 weeks from survey to commissioning, depending on system size and site conditions.
Is there any government subsidy available? +
Yes, various state and central government schemes offer subsidies for solar thermal systems. We assist with subsidy applications.
